WURFL database

Capabilites for developers

# Capability Description
1 device_os Identifies the operating system on the device. Useful for apps and determining compability due to differences between OS's like Android and iOS. Can be used for targetted advertising such as displaying other Apple products if the OS is iOS.
2 mobile_browser Identify the browser in use on the device. Could be useful in targetting certain browsers and ensuring no errors occur with differences on browser features/capabilities. Targetted advertising such as plugins/extensions for their browser or to convince switching to a different browser.
3 ram Returns amount of RAM the device has. Can determine if device can run larger apps that require larger amounts of RAM, or change app settings depending on available RAM. Knowing the available RAM allows a developer to adapt their app/plugin/extension to function quickly with what's available.
