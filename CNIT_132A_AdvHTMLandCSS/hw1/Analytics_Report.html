First report

Between the dates of Aug 26 to Sep 22 2021 for my Engagement Report and my Page Views Report, and Audiences Report, I noticed that traffic was sparse and engagement time was fairly low at only 51 seconds. Most traffic was on the 28th of August with very sparse views afterwards. With the Page Views and Audiences report I was able to see what type of users and where they were engaged more on my website. Half of users visited my assignments page, and only 5.9% visited my tips and tricks page. Max number of users in a single day was 4 on the 27th of August, and afterwards was only 1 user with a gap of a week or more afterwards. All users were from the United States. Of the total of 6 users that visited my website, 5 were on desktop and 1 was on mobile.

Users were mostly interested in what assignments I've done at that time. I should add more links to my Assignments page to keep them more engaged. Having more content on the landing page would give them more to interact with and increase engagement time. I would also need more users interacting with the site since total numbers of users was sparse, so sharing a link to more friends and family would give me more data. Having 1 out of 6 users on a mobile phone would mean I should make sure the site is more responsive and image even more compressed to keep load times short and less data usage for images.
