    CS 110B: Assignment 3 Announcement

    No unread replies. No replies.

    Hi everyone,

    This week we will be spending some time reviewing functions. For this assignment, there are three specific functions listed in the instructions that must be present in your code. If you feel like you could decompose these three functions further, you are free to do so. However, what I have listed is the minimum that should be in the code.

    From now on, it would be a good idea to look through the styling conventions in the syllabus before you submit your code to make sure you didn't miss anything that could cost you points. Now that we are working with functions, one of the big ones to pay attention to is styling convention 1C, which describes how to document your functions. If you have any questions about this styling convention, or any of the styling conventions in general, you can either contact me or post your questions on the class discussion board. If you post it on the discussion board for others to see or you are able to answer someone else's question, you will also get your participation credit for the week.

    Good luck and let me know if you need anything!

    Dave
